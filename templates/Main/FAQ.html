How to register an account?

There are three steps to register your account:

  1. Click 'Register' from the welcome page
  2. Enter your Email and password
  3. If it shows success, you've already created an account!

How to recharge?

There are five steps to recharge:

  1. Firstly, you should login to your account.
  2. Click 'Recharge' button.
